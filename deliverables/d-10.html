B. AI Production Pipeline — Human-Led, Geo-Accurate
  1. Base geometry: masterplan linework and 3D massing exported per view; all eight cameras locked in the model with site coordinates.
  2. AI mood frames: ControlNet (lineart + depth from the locked viewports, conditioning ≥0.8) drives diffusion for atmosphere only — geometry may not drift. Every prompt, seed and control image is logged to Annex 3.
  3. Human 3D verification: the design team rebuilds each approved mood frame as a physically-lit render and checks geometry, planting counts, shade coverage and lux against the model. No view is submitted that cannot be reproduced from the geo-accurate model — AI proposes atmosphere; humans decide and verify.

D. Additional Supporting Materials (#16)
  • Physical model 1:500 — CNC sand-toned base, terracotta loop inlay, removable canopy layer.
  • Interactive shade-simulation web viewer — hourly shadow slider, 21 Jun / 21 Dec presets, per-room shaded-area percentage.
  • AI-prompt transparency appendix (Annex 3) — the differentiator: every prompt, model version, seed and ControlNet input mapped to view codes V01–V08, plus rejected outputs and the human decision that overrode each — an auditable record that AI served the design, not the reverse.
  • Community survey annex — 312 resident responses, methodology, and how findings shaped the ten rooms and the dawn-to-night wellness programme.
