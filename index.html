The Living Shade Loop

One continuous shaded walk threads ten garden rooms through the real 15,001 m² Al Safa 2 parcel — a figure-eight plus perimeter ring designed by an AI-assisted workflow and verified with open, runnable evidence. This site is the team's working index: Design holds the plan, imagery and 3D; Submission holds the checklist, cost plan and every final document.
